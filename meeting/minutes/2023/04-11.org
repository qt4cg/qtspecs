+ MK: The ‚Äúfat arrow‚Äù operator is in 3.1 and proved very successful,

  + ‚Ä¶ I think a lot of this is based on the fact that ~=>~ didn‚Äôt do

    precedence. If you‚Äôre trying to chain, like ~$x -> f() -> {.+1}~, the precedence of the =!=
