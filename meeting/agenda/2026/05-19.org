** Group policy on commecial LLM-generated content
:PROPERTIES:
:CUSTOM_ID: llm-generated
:END:

PRs #2633-#2638 reflect the output of a commercial LLM. The community group
should decide, before discussing these items, what its policy is going to be on
LLM generated content. This is a policy choice that needs to be made first
because accepting them, or not, will /establish/ a policy.
